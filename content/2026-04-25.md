## Scripture

> 15 The eyes of all look to you, and you give them their food in due season.
> 16 You open your hand; you satisfy the desire of every living thing.

## Reflection

The Psalmist does not say God answers every demand. He says God opens his hand. There is a difference between hurry and provision, and the difference is the patience of *due season*.

Most of what we worry about is timing. Will it come? Will it come soon enough? The Psalm offers no schedule, only a posture: an open hand, and eyes that have learned to look up.

## Prayer

Teach me to wait with my eyes open. Give me bread for today, trust for tomorrow, and the patience to recognize your hand when it opens.
