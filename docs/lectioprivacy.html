Lectio Daily Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 25, 2026

Overview

Lectio Daily is designed to be a quiet, low-data devotional app. We do not require an account, and we do not sell personal information.

Information We Collect

Lectio Daily does not ask you to create an account and does not collect your name, email address, contacts, precise location, photos, or payment information.

The app may store preferences locally on your device, such as theme choice, text size, cached devotional content, and playback-related state. This information stays on your device unless you choose to remove the app or clear device data.

Network Requests

To load daily content, scripture, artwork, and audio, Lectio Daily makes network requests to content and media providers. These services may receive standard technical information such as your IP address, device browser or app request metadata, requested URLs, and timestamps.

  • GitHub may serve devotional JSON content.
  • Cloudflare R2 may serve artwork and audio files.
  • bible-api.com may be used to fetch Bible passage text.

Analytics and Tracking

Lectio Daily does not currently include third-party advertising, cross-app tracking, or analytics SDKs.

Children's Privacy

Lectio Daily is not designed to collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through a support channel, please contact us so it can be removed.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may use infrastructure providers only as needed to deliver app content, audio, artwork, scripture, and support.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as Lectio Daily changes. The latest version will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

For privacy or support questions, visit the Lectio Daily support email.
