## Description

Lectio Daily helps you return to Scripture with a calmer rhythm.

Each day brings together a focused Bible passage, a short written devotion, a prayer, and an audio reflection. The app is designed for ordinary mornings, quiet pauses, and the moments when you want to listen instead of scroll.

Lectio Daily includes:

- Daily Bible passages using the NET translation
- Written devotional reflections
- Guided prayer for each day
- Audio devotion playback
- Light and dark reading themes
- Adjustable text size
- Local caching for recently loaded content

Lectio Daily is intentionally simple. No account is required. No social feed competes for your attention. Just a daily place to read, pray, and listen.

Whether you have ten quiet minutes or only enough space to hear one passage, Lectio Daily is built to help Scripture feel less like a task and more like a return.

## App Review Notes Draft

Lectio Daily is a daily Bible study app. It loads devotional JSON from a public GitHub-hosted static content feed, streams artwork and audio from Cloudflare R2, and fetches scripture passages using a public Bible API with NET translation support. No account is required. No user-generated content or purchases are included in this build.

If today's content has not refreshed, the app may show bundled fallback devotional content so review can proceed without depending on a specific publication date.
